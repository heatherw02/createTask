Bầu cua cá cọp
To WIN you must have the most coins

HOW TO PLAY:
- Place as many coins as you want by clicking on one or more of the six images
- Once you finish placing your coin[s], next click [hold], then it's the next player turn
- Once everyone have placed their coin[s], click [roll] to roll the three dice
- If the dice lands on the animal, you placed your coins on, you get coins
- To determine how much coin[s] you get is: animal (# of dice of that specfic animal) * coins (you bet on)
Ex: 2 horse dice * 3 coins you bet on horse = 6 coins
- If you wish to end game click [end]

- Note: Game will end when user decides to end it or everyone have 0 coins left
